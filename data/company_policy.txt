VectorFlow Corporation - Employee Policy Manual

Section 1: Office Hours
The standard work hours for all VectorFlow employees are from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM, Monday through Thursday. Fridays are mandatory "no-meeting" days dedicated entirely to deep coding.

Section 2: Pet Policy
Employees are heavily encouraged to bring their pets to the office. However, iguanas are strictly prohibited in the server room due to the incident in 2024.

Section 3: Equipment Budget
Every new AI Engineer is allocated a $5,000 budget to purchase mechanical keyboards and ultra-wide monitors.
